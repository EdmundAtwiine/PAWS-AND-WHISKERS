Our Mission

We believe every paw deserves a home and every whisker deserves care

At Paws & Whiskers, we're dedicated to rescuing stray dogs and cats from the streets, providing them with medical care, and finding them loving forever homes. Since 2015, we've successfully rehomed over 2,000 animals and continue to work tirelessly to address the stray animal crisis in our community.

Our network of volunteers and foster families ensures that each animal receives the individual attention and care they need before finding their perfect match.
